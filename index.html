About Us

OTHERS Technology is a Silicon Valley VC-backed startup founded by PhDs from example University. We are building system sensor fusion solutions with our custom silicon for future autonomous driving and robotic applications.

When continued scaling is no longer possible, we believe new energy efficient architecture and advanced photonics I/O along with full-stack hardware/software co-design is the future to transform industries.

Our team is led by Dr. David Tan, who was a founding engineer of company example (NYSE:SPGT). You will be working in a small but very collaborative environment under guidance of industry veterans in the valley.
